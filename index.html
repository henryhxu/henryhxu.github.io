Research Interests

I work on systems and networking. I lead the NetX Lab. Welcome to visit us!

Our research is/was supported by funding from Research Grants Council (RGC) of Hong Kong, The Chinese University of Hong Kong, City University of Hong Kong, Huawei, Microsoft Research Asia, Azure, Amazon Web Services, etc.

Current Projects

  • ML/DA systems: machine learning and data analytics are important, but system and deployment support of ML/DA is often the bottleneck to real-world use

    • Sparse and adaptive computation, new computing paradigms must be explored to keep scaling our deep neural networks

    • Large-scale ML, faster and easier training and inference for large-scale ML workloads

    • Simulation, accurate and efficient performance simulation of distributed training

    • Resource scheduling, schedule jobs and coordinate resources with maximum efficiency

  • Networking: what would the future network look like?

    • Programmable dataplane, how can we better use the SmartNICs/DPUs?

    • ML-empowered network management, how can ML help reduce the human effort needed in various network management tasks?

    • NetKernel, re-architecting network stack as part of the virtualized infrastructure

  • Video analytics: pervasive edge devices are churning video contents at an exponential rate, and to keep up we need highly efficient video analytics pipelines
