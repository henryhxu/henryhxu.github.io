Research Interests

I work on computer networks and systems. I lead the NetX Lab. Welcome to visit us!

Current Projects

  • Data center transport: load balancing, congestion control, scheduling, virtualization

  • NFV frameworks: line-rate low-latency programmable packet processing with minimal resource footprint

  • ML/DA systems: machine learning and data analytics are important, but system and deployment support of ML/DA (resource allocation, scheduling) is often the bottleneck to real-world use
