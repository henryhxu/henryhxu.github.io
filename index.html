Research Interests

I work on systems and networking. I lead the NetX Lab. Welcome to visit us!

Our research is/was supported by funding from Research Grants Council (RGC) of Hong Kong, City University of Hong Kong, Huawei, Amazon Web Services, Microsoft Azure, etc.

Current Projects

  • Data center transport: what would the future data center network look like?

    • NetKernel, network stack as a service

  • ML/DA systems: machine learning and data analytics are important, but system and deployment support of ML/DA is often the bottleneck to real-world use

    • Resource scheduling, schedule jobs/tasks/executors with maximum efficiency

    • Distributed training, faster and easier distributed training for large-scale ML
