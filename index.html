Research Interests

I work on systems and networking. I lead the NetX Lab. Welcome to visit us!

Our research is/was supported by funding from Research Grants Council (RGC) of Hong Kong, The Chinese University of Hong Kong, City University of Hong Kong, Huawei, Microsoft Research Asia, Azure, Amazon Web Services, etc.

Current Projects

  • ML systems: ML/AI is important, but infra and deployment support is often the bottleneck to real-world use

    • Compound and complex AI systems, orchestrate the workflows of compound AI systems with more than the LLM, and complex AI systems with more than a linear reasoning path

    • Large-scale ML, faster, cheaper, and easier training and inference for large-scale ML workloads

    • Simulation, accurate and efficient performance simulation of distributed training

    • Related pubs: StitchLLM@ACL’25, Ayo@ASPLOS’25, Echo, ICPP’24, EMNLP’23, ATC’23, EuroSys’23, CoNEXT’18, SoCC’18

  • Networking: what would the future network look like?

    • Programmable dataplane, how can we better use the SmartNICs/DPUs?

    • ML-empowered network management, how can ML help reduce the human effort needed in network management tasks?

    • Related pubs: Yala@ASPLOS’25, Meili, NAssim@SIGCOMM’22, DQN@SIGCOMM’22, INFOCOM’24, CoNEXT’22, ATC’20, MM’23, INFOCOM’23

  • Learning: we also study theory and algorithm on the side

    • Learning to Optimize (L2O), how can we leverage DL to help solve optimization programs?

    • Related pubs: CVPR’24, INFOCOM’24
